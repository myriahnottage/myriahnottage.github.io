I think about lots of stuff, and sometimes I write about it.

"If you can't afford to live here, then leave"

I grew up in the SF Bay Area, from El Sobrante, to Richmond, to Oakland. I love the Bay with a passion, but I haven't lived there since 2018. First, I moved to Idaho because:

1. I could actually afford to buy a house on a generous $27/hour wage

2. I have family there who I grew up visiting every summer


Then, I moved to southern california for the weather and the politics -- a topic for it's own article. But when not move back to the bay area? I have amazing friends and family in the area, it's a place filled with nostalgia for me, and the shinning pinnacle of career growth in silicon valley.
